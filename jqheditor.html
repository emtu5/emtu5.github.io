Jewel Quest Heritage Board Editor

  • test1

Instructions

  • Select a tile type from the top-left with a click, then simply draw on the board.
  • Once you're satisfied with your layout, hit "Copy to Clipboard". This will copy the text required for the boards.cfge file.
  • On the right you may set additional parameters for the board (such as current jewels, time/expert time, etc.).

Notes

  • Although you can introduce tiles with id's 8 and C, they are not used in the vanilla game. A board with the former will crash as soon as it's loaded.
  • You cannot select more than 8 jewels on a board, as the game has only 8 slots for in-game jewels.
